## Common Praise

### Ease of Use
- Seamless navigation and creativity in video production
- Effortlessly transforming ideas into visuals and scripts
- Quick storyboarding and idea visualization

### Creative Control
- Granular fine-tuning that "puts the power of a director at your fingertips"
- Individual shot control, timing modification, visual element adjustment
- Consistent character appearances across scenes
- Closes gap between AI generation and professional video editing

### Character Consistency
- Praised for maintaining character and location details across shots
- One of the model's standout features vs. competitors

### Speed (Open-Source Model)
- Real-time generation on capable hardware
- Fastest open-source video generation model
- 30x faster than comparable models with multiscale rendering (13B)

### Open-Source Philosophy
- Day-one ComfyUI integration praised by creators
- Full model weights available for customization
- Community can train LoRAs and fine-tune

## Common Complaints

### Video Quality Variability
- Quality can vary between generations
- Surreal results and poor anatomy adherence compared to some competitors
- Prompt following depends heavily on prompting style

### Performance Issues
- Render speed slow during peak hours (LTX Studio cloud)
- High VRAM requirements for local generation (16-32GB+)
- macOS/AMD GPU support limited

### LTX Studio Billing/Service
- Pricing structure hard to justify for creators who don't use it constantly
- Reports of unauthorized yearly subscription charges
- Customer service complaints on Trustpilot
- Claims of non-existent or dishonest customer service
- Billing discrepancies reported

### Learning Curve
- Advanced features have a steep learning curve for beginners
- Need to prompt in very detailed and specific way for good results
- Confusion between LTX Studio vs. LTX Desktop vs. open-source model

### Technical Limitations
- Not designed for factual information
- May amplify existing societal biases
- Can generate inappropriate/offensive content
- Audio quality may be lower without speech (LTX-2)

## Feature Requests
- **Community Creations** gallery/showcase feature
- Better stability and error handling
- Clearer billing transparency
- Lower VRAM requirements
- Improved face/anatomy consistency
- Longer video generation capabilities
