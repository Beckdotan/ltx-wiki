## LTX-2 Audio To Video by multimodalart

### Overview
Apolinario (multimodalart) created a Space for generating lip-synced videos from images and audio clips.

### Key Details
- **Creator:** Apolinario / multimodalart
- **Likes:** 49
- **Status:** Running on Zero
- **Purpose:** Generate lip-synced video from an image and audio clip

### Significance
This Space demonstrates one of LTX-2's unique capabilities: generating video that is synchronized to input audio, enabling applications like:
- Talking head generation from a single photo
- Music video visualization
- Audio-reactive video content

## Use Cases Enabled

These projects showcase LTX-2/2.3's joint audio-video generation capability being applied to practical creative tools:
1. **Content creators** can animate portraits with lip-sync from audio
2. **Podcast/video producers** can generate talking head videos
3. **Creative professionals** can control video generation with first and last frame constraints
4. **Musicians** can generate audio-reactive visuals
