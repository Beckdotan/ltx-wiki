## Key Technical Capabilities Demonstrated

### Visual Quality
- Native 4K resolution output
- Up to 50 FPS frame rates
- Hyper-realistic lighting and textures
- Professional camera movements (dolly, crane, tracking)

### Control Features
- Camera movement direction with precision
- Keyframe-based motion definition
- Visual reference guidance
- Text rendering in videos (LTX-2.3 improvement)

### Audio-Visual Sync (LTX-2+)
- Synchronized dialogue generation
- Music generation matched to visuals
- Sound effects generation
- Up to 20-second clips with synchronized audio
