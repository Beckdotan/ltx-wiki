# LTX-Video License and Terms

**Source:** https://huggingface.co/Lightricks/LTX-Video
**Date:** 2026-04-13

## Overview

LTX-Video models are released under the **LTX-Video-Open-Weights License**, a custom open-weights license created by Lightricks. This is not a standard open-source license (not Apache 2.0, MIT, etc.).

---

## License Details

**Full license text:** https://huggingface.co/Lightricks/LTX-Video/blob/main/LTX-Video-Open-Weights-License-0.X.txt

### Key Points
- Allows both **research and commercial use**
- Requires **attribution** to Lightricks
- Custom license terms specific to AI model weights
- Applies to all model variants (13B, 2B, upscalers, ICLoRA adapters)

### Version Coverage
- v0.9.8+ models: LTX-Video-Open-Weights-License
- v0.9.6 models: Same license family

## Usage Restrictions

Based on the model card's stated limitations and intended use:

### Intended Uses
- Creative video generation
- Research and development
- Commercial applications (with attribution)
- Educational purposes

### Not Intended For
- Generation of factual/informational content (model may hallucinate)
- Creating deceptive or misleading content
- Any use that violates applicable laws
