# LTX Video Discord Community

## Overview
The LTX Discord server serves as the primary community hub for LTX Video users, developers, and creators. It provides real-time discussions, troubleshooting, and community-based exploration of the LTX ecosystem.

## Key Details
- **Server Name:** LTX (also known as LTX Studio)
- **Server ID:** 1206939519132114974
- **Invite Link:** https://discord.com/invite/ltxplatform
- **Alternative Invite:** https://discord.gg/ltxplatform
- **Members:** ~70,000+
- **Source:** https://discord.com/servers/ltx-1206939519132114974

## Role in the Ecosystem

### Community Support
- Real-time troubleshooting for LTX Video, LTX Desktop, ComfyUI integration
- Discussion of prompting techniques and workflow optimization
- Sharing of generated content and creative results

### Early Access and Beta Testing
- Special access opportunities for Discord community members
- Beta testing coordination for new features
- Exclusive early access spots during product launches (e.g., 200 exclusive spots during initial LTX Studio rollout)

### Developer Engagement
- Direct communication with Lightricks development team
- Feature request submissions and discussions
- Bug reporting and feedback collection

### Community for Open-Source Users
- LTX-Video-Trainer community support
- LoRA sharing and discussion
- ComfyUI workflow exchange
- Hardware optimization tips

## Historical Notes
- Discord community was actively used during LTX Studio beta launch (2024)
- Community grew significantly with open-source model releases
- Became central hub for open-source contributors alongside GitHub
