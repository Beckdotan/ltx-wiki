- **Founded:** January 2013
- **Headquarters:** Jerusalem, Israel
- **Additional Offices:** Haifa, London, New York, Chicago
- **Employees:** ~600
- **Valuation:** $1.8 billion (as of September 2021 Series D)
- **Total Funding:** $361 million

- **Monthly Paying Users:** 6.6 million+
- **Monthly Users:** 50 million+
- **Total App Downloads:** 730 million+
- **Website:** https://www.lightricks.com

## Product Portfolio

### Consumer Mobile Apps

**Facetune (March 2013)**
- Selfie-retouching photo and video editor
- Named App Store's Best of 2013
- 200 million+ downloads (as of 2023)
- Features: teeth whitening, blemish removal, reshaping, hair try-on, filters, background blur/swap, AI headshots
- Available on iOS and Android

**Videoleap**
- Mobile video editor
- Named Apple's 2017 App of the Year
- Brought previously complex video editing flows to mobile
- Features: professional video editing tools, effects, transitions
- Target: content creators for Instagram, YouTube, TikTok

**Photoleap (formerly Photofox/Enlight)**
- General image editing app
- One of the first mobile apps to offer generative AI (text-to-image) capabilities
- Features: artwork generation, social media content creation, multiple editing options

**Facetune Video**
- Selfie-retouching video tool
- Real-time retouching and editing of selfie/portrait videos using AI

### Professional AI Platform (LTX Ecosystem)

**LTX Studio (February 2024)**
- Web-based AI video production platform
- Script-to-video, storyboarding, character consistency, camera controls
- Multiple pricing tiers ($0-$125/month)
- Website: https://ltx.studio

**LTX Desktop (March 2026)**
- Open-source desktop video production app
- Local AI inference on consumer hardware
- Apache 2.0 license
- GitHub: https://github.com/Lightricks/LTX-Desktop

**LTX Models (LTXV / LTX-2)**
- Open-source video generation foundation models
- LTX-2.3 is latest (22B parameters, 4K, 50fps, synchronized audio)
- Available on HuggingFace and GitHub

**LTX API**
- Developer API for programmatic video generation
- Per-second billing, multiple resolution tiers
- Documentation: https://docs.ltx.video

**ComfyUI-LTXVideo**
- Custom nodes for ComfyUI integration
- GitHub: https://github.com/Lightricks/ComfyUI-LTXVideo

**LTX-Video-Trainer**
- LoRA and full fine-tuning toolkit
- GitHub: https://github.com/Lightricks/LTX-Video-Trainer

### Creator Platform

**Popular Pays (acquired 2022)**
- Influencer and creator marketing platform
- Helps creators monetize content
- Bridges Lightricks' mobile apps with brand partnerships
- Offices in Chicago

## AI Strategy

In summer 2022, with the emergence of generative AI, Lightricks began incorporating GenAI into its apps (Facetune, Photoleap), becoming one of the first to offer text-to-image on mobile.

The LTX brand represents Lightricks' professional/enterprise AI initiative:
- **ltx.io** - Central hub for LTX platform, models, API, developer tools, enterprise
- **ltx.studio** - Commercial video creation platform
- **ltx.dev** - Developer-focused model access
