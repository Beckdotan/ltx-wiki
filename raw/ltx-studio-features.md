# LTX Studio - AI Video Creation Platform

## Overview

LTX Studio is a full AI video production platform developed by Lightricks, launched in February 2024 and opened to the public in August 2024. It is a web-based platform (no download required) that turns creative ideas into high-quality, professional videos, simplifying every stage from scripting and storyboarding to editing and final delivery.

**Website:** https://ltx.studio

## Target Audience

- **Filmmakers & Production Teams** - pre-production visualization, storyboarding, shot planning
- **Marketing & Advertising Professionals** - campaign videos, social media content, product launches
- **Creative Agencies** - concept validation, client pitches, rapid iteration
- **Content Creators** - YouTube, Instagram, TikTok content at professional grade
- **Enterprise Teams** - branded content at scale with collaboration features

## Core Features

### Script-to-Video Pipeline
- Upload or write scripts that are automatically divided into scenes and shots
- AI extracts characters and objects as reusable "Elements"
- Choice between FLUX or Nano Banana image models for storyboard generation
- Set aspect ratio upfront for all shots
- Script-to-visual alignment maintained throughout

### Storyboard Generator
- Completely rebuilt for speed and creative control (5x faster than previous versions)
- Shot-by-shot visual generation from scripts
- Reusable elements system for consistency

### Character Consistency (Persistent Character Profiles)
- Define a character's age, ethnicity, hairstyle, wardrobe, and facial details once
- System maintains exact appearance across every shot in the project
- "Trained Actors" system for pre-built consistent characters
- Saved Elements for custom character persistence

### Advanced Camera Controls
- Set keyframes for crane lifts
- Orbit subjects in 3D space
- Execute tracking shots
- Sketch objects onto the canvas
- Professional camera motion presets: dolly, crane, pan, handheld
- Rivals professional pre-visualization software

### Audio Design
- SFX and soundtrack generation without leaving the platform
- Music and ambient audio generated to match scene mood
- Voice-over support for narration
- Audio stays consistent across all generations
- Can change camera angles, adjust visuals, or modify scenes while keeping dialogue or music locked

### Video Generation Models
- Supports multiple AI video generation models
- Veo 2 model (available from Standard tier)
- Veo 3.1 model (available from Pro tier)
- LTX's own models

### Animatics
- Convert storyboards to animatics
- Pre-visualization tool for film and advertising

### Collaboration
- Project collaboration (up to 3 collaborators per project on Pro plan)
- Pitch deck generation
- Team workflows

### Export & Output
- Multiple resolution options
- Commercial-use licensing (from Standard tier and above)

## Pricing (as of 2026)

### Free Plan
- 800 credits (one-time, not monthly)
- Personal use license only (no commercial use)

### Lite Plan
- **$15/month** (or $12/month billed yearly)
- 8,640 credits per month
- Personal use only

### Standard Plan
- **$35/month** (or $28/month billed yearly)
- 28,000 credits per month
- Commercial use licensing
- Veo 2 model access
- Trained actors
- Accelerated rendering
- Pitch decks

### Pro Plan
- **$125/month** (or $100/month billed yearly)
- 110,000 credits per month
- Veo 3.1 video model
- Project collaboration (up to 3 collaborators)
- All Standard features

### Enterprise Plan
- Custom pricing
- Unlimited credits
- Custom model training
- SSO, GDPR/ISO/SOC 2 compliance

### Credit System
- Credits are consumed when generating videos and images
- Different actions consume different amounts of credits
- A 10-second video in 720p costs approximately 20 credits
- A 10-second video in 1080p costs approximately 40 credits
