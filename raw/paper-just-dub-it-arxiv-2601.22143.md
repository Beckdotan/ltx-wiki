## Key Details (from AVControl references)

This paper uses the AVControl framework for video dubbing via joint audio-visual diffusion. It demonstrates the extensibility of the AVControl/LTX-2 ecosystem for practical creative applications.

## Significance to LTX Ecosystem

- Built directly on the AVControl framework and LTX-2 backbone
- Demonstrates the modular LoRA approach for video dubbing
- Shows that the parallel canvas conditioning mechanism generalizes beyond the original set of trained modalities
- Key authors overlap with LTX-2 and AVControl teams
- Collaboration between Lightricks and Tel Aviv University

## Notes

This paper was identified through the AVControl paper (reference [9]) as a concurrent work that adopted the AVControl framework. It represents a downstream application of the LTX-2 model family for the specific task of video dubbing -- generating synchronized lip movements and audio for video content.
