## Curious Refuge - "LTX 2 | An Honest AI Video Generator Review"

- **URL**: https://curiousrefuge.com/blog/ltx2-ai-video-generator-review
- **Date**: 2026
- **Affiliation**: Non-biased, non-affiliated review

### Scores (out of 10)
- **Visual Fidelity**: 7.3 - "a standout strength"
- **Motion Coherence**: 5.8
- **Prompt Adherence**: 6.3
- **Overall assessment**: LTX 2 has made "the biggest waves in the AI Video world due to its impressive quality outputs as well as it being an open source model"

### Key Findings
- **Prompt adherence strength**: "LTX's greatest strength in prompt adherence lies in its literalism -- as long as the direction is physical and rooted in the camera, the model performs with near-mechanical precision"
- **Visual quality**: "Achieves its best fidelity in controlled conditions: one action, one subject, one light. The dynamic range holds without clipping, and tonal separation between horizon and sky feels nearly photographic"
- **Edge quality**: "Edges of the image stay crisp without the usual shimmer or compression artifacts seen in older diffusion systems"
- **Motion limitation**: "Motion in LTX is precise, disciplined, and strangely unemotional -- the transformer architecture that keeps LTX's frames clean also governs how it moves"
- **Semantic gap**: "In almost every test, LTX follows the nouns of a prompt more faithfully than the verbs -- it can place the 'yellow trolley,' the 'wet city street,' and the 'hazy light' exactly as described, but struggles once the language shifts to ambiguity"
- **Missing emotion**: "Where LTX keeps missing is intention and emotion"

## WaveSpeedAI Blog - "LTX-2.3 vs WAN 2.2 Comparison"

- **URL**: https://wavespeed.ai/blog/posts/ltx-2-3-vs-wan-2-2-comparison-2026/
- **Date**: 2026

### Key Findings
- Speed: LTX-2.3 claimed 18x faster than WAN 2.2-14B on H100 (unverified independently)
- Practical test: 5-second 720p clip in under 1 minute (LTX) vs 4-5 minutes (WAN 2.2)
- Quality: WAN 2.2 motion has "physical weight," LTX 2.3 slightly more "animated"
- Audio: "LTX 2.3 wins on audio -- native audio generation built into the same pass as video"
- Conclusion: "LTX-2.3 is built for speed and output stability; WAN 2.2 leans into cinematic control"

## CrePal Content Center - "LTX 2.3 vs WAN 2.2: Best Open-Source Video Model in 2026?"

- **URL**: https://crepal.ai/blog/aivideo/ltx-2-3-vs-wan-2-2/
- **Date**: 2026

### Key Findings
- LTX-2.3: "First open-weights model to credibly remove that asterisk across multiple dimensions at once"
- Biggest practical improvement: "motion consistency - LTX 2 had a tendency to drift with background elements subtly shifting position, while LTX 2.3 holds scenes tighter"
- Portrait improvement: "faces generated in vertical format had a mushy, low-detail quality in LTX 2 that's mostly fixed in 2.3"
- Most consequential change: "rebuilt VAE with redesigned latent space that results in sharper fabric, cleaner hair, and stable chrome reflections during camera moves"
