## Platform Overview
LTX Studio is distinct from the open-source LTX Video model. It is Lightricks' commercial AI video production platform built on top of the LTX models, offering a full creative pipeline from script to final scene.

### Key Platform Features
- **Script-to-video** generation
- **Storyboarding** with AI-generated frames
- **Character consistency** across shots via Persistent Character Profiles
- **Professional camera controls**
- **Timeline editor**
- **Scene-based thinking** (thinks in scenes and sequences, not isolated shots)

### Persistent Character Profiles
- Define character's age, ethnicity, hairstyle, wardrobe, facial details once
- System maintains exact appearance across every shot
- "Solves arguably the hardest problem in AI video: keeping characters looking the same from scene to scene"

## LTX Studio vs Open-Source LTX Video
- **LTX Studio**: Commercial platform, cloud-based, full production pipeline, subscription model
- **LTX Video/LTX-2**: Open-source model, local generation, requires technical setup
- **LTX Desktop**: Open-source app bridging the gap (local generation with GUI)
