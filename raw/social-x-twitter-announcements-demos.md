## Official Announcements

### LTX Video Initial Launch (November 2024)
- **@LTXStudio**: "(1/13) We've been working on something special. Introducing LTX Video, Lightricks' new open-source, community-driven model for video generation. Create breathtaking videos in moments, blazing past traditional playback speeds--this is LTX Video."
- **@Lightricks**: "Today we're proud to introduce LTXV - our groundbreaking open-source AI video generation model that creates stunning videos faster than you can watch them."
- 13-part thread detailing capabilities and technical specs

### LTX-2 Open Source Release (January 2026)
- **@ltx_model**: "AI video shouldn't be locked behind closed systems. We're releasing LTX-2 as a truly open-source AI video model. Here's @ZeevFarbman (CEO & Co-Founder, Lightricks) on why openness, local access, and community matter."
- CEO Zeev Farbman personally championed the open-source approach
- Emphasized community-driven development philosophy

## Influencer/Creator Coverage

### Rowan Cheung (@rowancheung) - The Rundown AI
- "Lightricks just announced LTXV, the unicorn's new AI video model"
- "What stands out is that it's the first AI video model that can generate high-quality real-time video, and it's open source"
- Highlighted specs: 5 seconds of 24 FPS medium res (768x512) in 4 seconds
- Noted: runs on consumer-grade NVIDIA RTX 4090
- Mentioned: 2 billion parameter Diffusion Transformer
- Noted availability on HuggingFace and ComfyUI

### Machine Delusions (@Machinedelusion)
- Covered LTX-2 technical specs:
  - Native 4K video at up to 50 FPS
  - High-quality long-form video with strong temporal consistency
  - Audio-conditioned generation with motion synced to sound
  - Fine-grained camera and motion control
  - Efficient enough to run on consumer GPUs

### Wes Roth (@WesRothMoney)

- Emphasized: native 4K at 50 FPS, synchronized audio + video
