## Key Prompting Principles (from Community Guides)
- Think cinematically using professional camera language
- Show emotion through physical actions rather than describing emotions
- Connect actions with temporal connectors for smooth progression
- Use detailed, specific prompts for best quality
- Describe lighting, textures, and environment in prompts
