# Pika Labs

Pika Labs is an AI video generation platform focused on accessibility, creative effects, and rapid iteration for social media content. It has carved out a niche in short-form social content creation with its unique suite of creative transformation tools.

## Model Versions

### Pika 2.2 (February 2025)
- 720p and 1080p resolution
- 5-10 second video generation
- Improved motion consistency and visual clarity

### Pika 2.5 (2026)
- Enhanced prompt adherence
- Popular for social media content at $8/month entry point

## Key Features

### Creative Effects Suite
- **Pikaframes:** Keyframe transition control -- upload start/end images, AI generates transition (1-10 seconds)
- **Pikaswaps:** Replace objects in video with full lighting and motion preservation
- **Pikadditions:** Insert new characters/objects into existing footage
- **Pikaffects:** Creative effects (inflate, melt, explode, squish, turn to cake)
- **Pikascenes:** Full scene generation from single text/image input

These creative transformation tools have no direct equivalent on competing platforms.

### Generation Capabilities
- Text-to-video, image-to-video, video-to-video
- Camera direction via text prompts (pans, zooms, tracking, orbital)
- 720p to 1080p output, 3-10 seconds per clip
- Generation speed: 15-30 seconds per clip (3-5x faster than [[competitor-runway|Runway]] or [[competitor-kling|Kling]])

### Adobe Integration
Pika 2.2 is available within Adobe Firefly's video editor.

## Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Price | Credits/Month |
|------|-------|---------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 80 |
| **Standard** | $10/mo | 700 |
| **Pro** | $35/mo | 2,300 |
| **Fancy** | $95/mo | 6,000 |

Commercial use restricted to Pro ($35/mo) and Fancy ($95/mo) plans only. Per-clip cost approximately $0.20-$0.45 for a 5-second video.

## Strengths
- Unique creative transformation effects (Pikaffects, Pikaswaps, Pikadditions) with no equivalent elsewhere
- Fastest generation speed for social content (15-30 seconds)
- Lowest entry price for casual users ($10/mo)
- Mobile-first, consumer-friendly interface
- Adobe Firefly ecosystem integration

## Weaknesses
- Lower quality ceiling than [[competitor-runway|Runway]], [[competitor-sora|Sora]], or [[competitor-veo|Veo]] -- optimized for social, not cinematic
- Short video duration (3-10 seconds maximum)
- No native audio generation
- No storyboarding or multi-scene workflow (individual clip generator only)
- No [[character-consistency]] across scenes
- No local deployment (cloud-only)
- No commercial rights on lower tiers
- Variable credit costs make budgeting difficult

## Comparison to LTX Studio

Pika and [[ltx-studio]] serve fundamentally different use cases. Pika excels at rapid, creative short-form social content with unique transformation effects. [[ltx-studio]] is a full production environment for multi-scene narrative content. Most teams would use Pika for social media volume and LTX Studio for professional production, making them complementary rather than directly substitutable.
