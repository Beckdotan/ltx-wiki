# Lightricks

Lightricks is an Israeli AI-first technology company that develops creative tools for mobile and professional video production. Founded in January 2013, it is headquartered in Jerusalem with additional offices in Haifa, London, New York, and Chicago.

## Key Metrics

- **Founded:** January 2013
- **Employees:** ~600
- **Valuation:** $1.8 billion (September 2021 Series D)
- **Total Funding:** $361 million
- **ARR:** $250 million (as of July 2025)
- **Monthly Paying Users:** 6.6 million+
- **Monthly Users:** 50 million+
- **Total App Downloads:** 730 million+

## Product Portfolio

### Consumer Mobile Apps

- **Facetune** (March 2013) - Selfie-retouching photo and video editor. App Store Best of 2013. 200 million+ downloads.
- **Videoleap** - Mobile video editor. Apple's 2017 App of the Year.
- **Photoleap** (formerly Photofox/Enlight) - General image editing app with early generative AI (text-to-image) capabilities.
- **Facetune Video** - Real-time AI retouching for selfie/portrait videos.

### Professional AI Platform

The [[ltx-ecosystem]] represents Lightricks' pivot from consumer mobile apps to professional-grade AI video production:

- [[ltx-studio]] - Web-based AI video production platform
- [[ltx-desktop]] - Open-source desktop video production app
- [[ltx-2-overview]] - Open-source video generation foundation models
- [[ltx-api]] - Developer API for programmatic video generation
- [[ltx-integration-projects]] - ComfyUI nodes, fine-tuning toolkit, third-party integrations

### Creator Platform

- **Popular Pays** (acquired 2022) - Influencer and creator marketing platform. Bridges Lightricks' mobile apps with brand partnerships. Offices in Chicago.

## AI Strategy

In summer 2022, Lightricks began incorporating generative AI into its apps, becoming one of the first to offer text-to-image on mobile. The LTX brand represents the professional/enterprise AI initiative:

- **ltx.io** - Central hub for platform, models, API, developer tools, enterprise
- **ltx.studio** - Commercial video creation platform
- **ltx.dev** - Developer-focused model access
