# LipSync and Portrait Animation

Multiple community members have built HuggingFace Spaces showcasing [[ltx-2-overview|LTX-2]] and [[ltx-2.3-model|LTX-2.3]]'s joint audio-video generation capability for portrait animation and lip-synchronization.

## LTX 2.3 Sync (linoyts)

- **Space:** https://huggingface.co/spaces/linoyts/LTX-2.3-Sync
- **Creator:** linoyts
- **Likes:** 120
- **Runtime:** HuggingFace Zero
- **Purpose:** Portrait animation and lip-sync with LTX-2.3

### Related Spaces by linoyts

- **LTX 2.3 First-Last Frame** -- 96 likes, creates short videos from text, images, and audio with first/last frame control
- **LTX-2 First Last Frame** -- 37 likes, earlier version for LTX-2

## Audio to Video (multimodalart)

- **Space:** https://huggingface.co/spaces/multimodalart/Ltx2-Audio-To-Video
- **Creator:** Apolinario (multimodalart)
- **Likes:** 49
- **Runtime:** HuggingFace Zero
- **Purpose:** Generate lip-synced video from an image and audio clip

This Space demonstrates generating video that is synchronized to input audio, enabling talking head generation from a single photo.

## Use Cases

These projects apply LTX-2/2.3's joint audio-video generation to practical creative tools:

1. **Content creators** -- Animate portraits with lip-sync from audio input
2. **Podcast/video producers** -- Generate talking head videos from a still image
3. **Creative professionals** -- Control video generation with first and last frame constraints
4. **Musicians** -- Generate audio-reactive visuals from music
