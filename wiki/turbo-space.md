# Turbo Space

**alexnasa** created "LTX-2 Video [Turbo]," the most popular community HuggingFace Space for [[ltx-2-overview|LTX-2]], which provides fast high-quality video with audio generation using Flash Attention 3 (FA3) optimization.

## Key Details

- **Creator:** alexnasa
- **Space:** https://huggingface.co/spaces/alexnasa/LTX-2-Video-Turbo
- **Likes:** 398 (highest among community LTX-2 Spaces)
- **Runtime:** HuggingFace Zero (free GPU tier)
- **Key optimization:** Flash Attention 3 for faster inference

## Significance

This Space demonstrates how community members can optimize LTX-2 inference beyond the default configuration. The "Turbo" designation indicates optimized inference speed while maintaining quality, achieved primarily through FA3.

## Derivatives and Clones

The success of the original Turbo Space spawned multiple derivatives:

| Creator | Space | Likes | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|-------|
| ZeroCollabs | LTX-2.3 Video [Turbo] | 40 | Upgraded to [[ltx-2.3-model|LTX-2.3]] |
| rbeard0330 | LTX-2 Video [Turbo] | 4 | Clone of original |
| Phamthihong | LTX-2.3 Turbo | 3 | LTX-2.3 variant |
| rahul7star | LTX-2.3 Turbo | 3 | LTX-2.3 variant |

This pattern of rapid replication and adaptation across the ecosystem is a common dynamic in the LTX community: a successful optimization approach is quickly ported to newer model versions by multiple creators.
