# X/Twitter LTX Video Announcements and Community Reaction

X (formerly Twitter) served as the primary announcement channel for LTX Video milestones and a major hub for community demos and workflow sharing.

## Official Announcements

### LTX Video Initial Launch (November 2024)

@LTXStudio published a 13-part thread introducing LTX Video:

> "(1/13) We've been working on something special. Introducing LTX Video, Lightricks' new open-source, community-driven model for video generation."

@Lightricks simultaneously announced: "Today we're proud to introduce LTXV -- our groundbreaking open-source AI video generation model that creates stunning videos faster than you can watch them."

### LTX-2 Open Source Release (January 2026)

@ltx_model announced the open-source release with a direct statement from CEO Zeev Farbman:

> "AI video shouldn't be locked behind closed systems. We're releasing LTX-2 as a truly open-source AI video model."

The CEO's personal championing of open source resonated strongly with the AI community on X.

## Influencer Coverage

### Rowan Cheung (@rowancheung) -- The Rundown AI

Highlighted LTX Video as "the first AI video model that can generate high-quality real-time video, and it's open source." Noted key specs: 5 seconds of 24 FPS at 768x512 in 4 seconds, running on consumer RTX 4090.

### Machine Delusions (@Machinedelusion)

Detailed LTX-2 technical specs: native 4K at up to 50 FPS, audio-conditioned generation with motion synced to sound, fine-grained camera and motion control, consumer GPU compatibility.

### Wes Roth (@WesRothMoney)

Called LTX-2 "a production-grade multimodal video generation model" and emphasized native 4K at 50 FPS with synchronized audio.

### Apolinario (@multimodalart) -- HuggingFace

Shared direct links to model weights, GitHub, and official demo, driving technical community engagement.

### Poonam Soni (@CodeByPoonam)

Listed LTXV-13B among top AI launches, calling it "the leading open-source video model, excelling in quality, speed, and accessibility."

## Community Activity on X

### Demo Sharing

- Early demos showcased cinematic clips from animated scenes to realistic narratives
- All LTX-2+ demos included integrated audio
- NVIDIA optimization reports showed 3x faster inference on RTX cards
- Community shared [[comfyui-ltx-workflows|ComfyUI workflow]] screenshots and results

### LTX 2.3 ComfyUI Workflow Virality

The LTX 2.3 ComfyUI workflow saw "explosive popularity on X and AI subreddits." Top creators validated the two-stage sampling approach, generating significant discussion around low-VRAM optimization strategies.

## Lightricks x Banodoco Hackathon

Lightricks ran an ADOS hackathon where teams of engineers and artists collaborated to push the open-source model to its limits. Results shared on X generated additional community interest.

## Sentiment

Sentiment on X is overwhelmingly positive for LTX Video announcements. The platform's engagement pattern shows:

- Strong metrics on announcement threads
- Speed consistently highlighted as the standout feature by influencers
- Open-source approach praised across the AI community
- [[ltx-audio-video-community-reception|Audio-video synchronization]] in LTX-2 generated significant buzz
- CEO Zeev Farbman's personal involvement in open-source advocacy appreciated
